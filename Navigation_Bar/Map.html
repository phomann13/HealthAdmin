Map


Welcome to our Map feature that showcases essential healthcare resources in your area. By utilizing this interactive map, you can easily locate primary care physicians, urgent care centers, and the Doctors Community Hospital.

If you click on the rectangle icon in the upper-left hand corner of the map, it will bring up a list of the primary care centers and urgent care centers in the area surrounding Doctors Community Hospital. Clicking on any pinpoint on the map will not only provide you with detailed information about the selected location but also offer convenient directions to that site. Whether you need a routine check-up or require immediate medical attention, our map feature ensures that you can find the nearest healthcare facility with ease!



How to read the Map:

Yellow pinpoint = Doctors Community Hospital
Blue pinpoints = Urgent Care Centers
Orange pinpoints = Primary Care Offices
